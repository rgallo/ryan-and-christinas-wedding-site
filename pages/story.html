Our Story

It was the end of Summer 2010, and so many exciting things were going on: Apple’s first iPad release, Despicable Me hitting theatres, the FIFA World Cup vuvuzela craze, and the beginning of both Christina and Ryan’s last year at Binghamton University. Christina would be finishing up her senior year of undergraduate, and Ryan his last year towards his MBA.

Christina and Ryan had both attended Binghamton for years, but it wasn’t until October 2, 2010 that their paths would finally cross. They both had plans to attend the same party at University Plaza, at Christina’s friend Peter’s apartment. As soon as Ryan walked in and Christina knew she didn’t recognize him, she decided to find out exactly why he was there:

“Who do you know here?”
“Uhm, I know Keith”
“Keith… who do you know here?”
“I’m friends with Sarah”

Now that it was confirmed that Ryan and Keith knew at least one of Christina’s friends, it was safe to chat. They spent the night talking about Binghamton, TV Shows, the LSAT Christina just finished taking the morning before, and the cool new photobooth app Ryan had just downloaded.

They exchanged numbers at the end of the night, and began speaking regularly right away, but it wasn’t until December 4, 2010 that their friendship turned into a relationship.
